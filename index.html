About Us

We tackle Digital Poverty head on by collect donated computers, tablets and smartphones, we securely erase the data, refurbish the devices and then give them to those who need help.


This includes individuals, charities, community groups, education centres and more.

What we do

We collect donated technology, securely erase the data and refurbish the devices (and safely recycle those we can’t!). Then we gift them to organisations or individuals in need. To Learn more about the journey of your unwanted tech please click here.

What is digital poverty?


"The inability to interact with the online world fully, when where and how an individual needs to."

42%

of young people are not adequately connected, by either lacking broadband or a computer.

of low income households have no access to the internet.
